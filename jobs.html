Join our Team!


At Maestro eSports, you are surrounded by some of the brightest minds in gaming. Our structure is flat, meaning that your ideas and opinions are constantly not only in demand but also heard. Each day will be met with complex and challenging tasks we hope to solve together. Quick decision making allows us to create products which shape and define the nature of the eSports market. We value team chemistry and team work above all else and have a friendly and fast paced work environment.



Designer's


Maestro Esports is a leading worldwide multiplatform Esports organization.


We are looking for skilled and creative people to help us achive our vision. Ideally you should have experience working with groups through multiple applications including Discord, Trello, Google docs/drive and plenty others. A Background in Esports is helpfull aswell, even if it is just a general gaming background.


DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Consistent with designs

  • Ability to meet deadlines

  • Self-motivated & reliable

  • Ability to follow branding guidelines

  • Excellent skill in preferred animation software

  • Own the tools you already need for motion design

  • Ability to give and recieve constructive feedback

  • 3+ years of experience of motion design (or show equivalent skill level)


  • Experience with illustrations

  • Experience with making emotes (eg. for Twitch streamers)

  • Experience with space themed motion designs

  • Experience in working with a team of creatives


Would you like to apply for this job?

Do you feel ready to work at Maestro Esports? Send us your application with your earliest starting date (including: CV and if available, some previous work samples & references) preferably in PDF format.

Also send a short e-mail introduction - what role you are interested in and why to: jobs@maestroesports.com
